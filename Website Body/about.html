Our Story

From a WhatsApp Group to a Continental Movement

What began as informal sessions teaching young Kenyans how to safely use mobile money evolved into a growing digital and financial literacy movement reaching communities across Africa.

Today, Digi-Fin Literacy Africa collaborates with schools, SACCOs, fintech companies, NGOs, youth groups and entrepreneurs to make modern financial tools accessible, practical and understandable.

We believe Africa's next economic leap will be driven by digitally fluent citizens making confident financial decisions every day.

Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower youth, individuals and businesses across Africa with practical digital literacy, financial knowledge, cybersecurity awareness and data competencies needed to participate successfully in the modern economy.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive, digitally literate and financially empowered Africa where every individual and enterprise possesses the skills, confidence and opportunities required to thrive in the digital economy.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Why It Matters

Closing the Digital & Financial Gap in Africa

As Africa becomes increasingly digital, financial opportunities are expanding rapidly. Yet millions remain excluded due to limited access to practical digital and financial education.

Digi-Fin Literacy Africa exists to ensure no one is left behind in the digital economy. Every workshop we run, every skill we teach, and every community we reach brings us closer to a financially empowered continent.
